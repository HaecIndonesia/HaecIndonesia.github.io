About Us

PT Haec Indonesia is a company engaged in electronics. Making electronic products such as computers, televisions, laptops and other electronic devices. Not only in the field of electronic devices, PT Haec Indonesia also issues accessories products - accessories related to electronics.

History

PT Haec Indonesia was established in 2016. It was founded by Harun, a high spirited person living in Depok, Indonesia. PT Haec Indonesia stands for Harun Electronic Indonesia. Moves in the field of electronics and has a target of creating electronic devices made in Indonesia that are able to compete internationality.

Until now, PT Haec Indonesia has employed more than 300 people from all over Indonesia and created more than 1000 electronic devices that have been distributed domestically and abroad.
